VA - NEW ANXIETIES

2019 AD. The Arctic Circle and the Amazon are on fire, fascism is on the rise again, the gap between the richest and the poorest is still widening. New anxieties need to be addressed.

Crux Axul has invited seven musicians to write and record a piece of music motivated by the way they perceive the world changing around them at a seemingly ever-increasing pace.

From Silicone’s flickering exercise in melodic modular experimentation, to YS’ contemporary take on Musique Concrète, to Shannon Soundquist’s longform drone meditation, New Anxieties offers seven takes on a world in turmoil that are each as unique as they are coherent as a whole.

Mastered by Simon Hawemann at Sludge Studios
Except "S2HG" mixed and mastered by Antonio Gallucci
Artwork by Sarah Kowalewski

Released October 31, 2019
